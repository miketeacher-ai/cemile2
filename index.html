Writing feedback codes

CodeMeaningTeacher action

CCCoherence and CohesionMark topic sentence, logical link and paragraph purpose.
LRLexical ResourceCircle vague words and request precise topic vocabulary.
GRAGrammar Range and AccuracyUnderline grammar errors that affect clarity first.

Speaking feedback codes

CodeMeaningTeacher action
FFluency and coherenceCheck answer length, pauses, linking and development.
VVocabularyNote repeated words and give stronger natural alternatives.
GGrammarTrack tense control, sentence range and self-correction.
PPronunciationMark word stress, final consonants, chunking and clarity.

Simple 5-point teacher score for every submitted task

ScoreMeaningNext action
1Incomplete or not timed.Redo under exact conditions.
2Completed but many instructions ignored.Repeat with teacher modelling.
3Band 5-level attempt: understandable but limited.Correct and upgrade one section.
4Approaching Band 6.5: clear but not consistently flexible.Add precision, examples and error control.
5Band 7 direction: clear, developed, controlled and exam-aware.Increase speed and complexity.
